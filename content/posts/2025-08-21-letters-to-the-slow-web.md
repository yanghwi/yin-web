The slow web is less a destination and more a habit. It is the choice to build
products that respect circadian rhythms, craft that rewards revisiting, and
communities that do not equate immediacy with care.

These letters sketch rituals for building and maintaining such spaces. They are
reminders that every notification we design is an invitation to borrow time from
someone else.
