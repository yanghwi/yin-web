title: "On automation's gendered risk"
date: "2025-10-02"
tags:

excerpt: "Care work is a blind spot for automation narratives."
---

Automation is rarely neutral. When the invisible labor of maintenance becomes a
metric, it is most often women and marginalized communities who are asked to do
more with less support. We call it efficiency and forget that efficiency for one
group can be fragility for another.

A humane future of work starts with naming these hidden costs, distributing the
benefits of tooling, and allowing for redundancy where care is at stake.
